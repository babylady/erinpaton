Where it Began—

With the company providing a fully-automated end-to-end marketing service, I was required to demonstrate a nuanced understanding of various internal and EAS software, maintain active involvement in growth and scale endeavours, develop marketing stack architecture, formulate/adapt products concurrent with emerging technologies, as well as work closely with developers and IT specialists (without whom the aforementioned would not be possible). I certainly got more than I bargained for but the simple misinterpretation of a recruitment ad facilitated valuable insights into some of the applications of information technology that perhaps a more executional marketing position would not have afforded and I feel fortunate to have stumbled into the world of technology as a result.
